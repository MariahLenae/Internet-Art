Pentel Student Brand Ambassador

I have been a brand ambassador for Pentel through the Youth Marketing Connection for the past two years. In this role, I host events and create social media content to promote Pentel as a brand and their products. For the events, my goal is to create spaces that encourage students to test out the products and inspire their creativity. Some events I have put on include creating motivational notecards to place around campus and coloring holiday cards to donate to a children’s hospital. For social media, I create multiple types of content to showcase the products, such as videos showcasing my artistic use of the products, posts showing how to incorporate the products in schoolwork, and images of how to use the products to create DIY cards! This has been such a fun position to combine my passion for art and marketing and work more on my digital and social media skills!

Internet Art Projects

Internet Art is a class I took for my Studio Art Minor, which includes the integration of image, video, animation, and audio for the creation of internet art. In other words, it was an introductory programming class that taught the basics of html, CSS, JavaScript, and p5.js. Each week, I wrote a blog post about what I had learned in the class or something outside of class that I had done that week. In addition to this, I created 5 exercises throughout the semester to test out different programming skills we had learned. Lastly, for my final project, I created this website that you’re on right now…hope you like it :)
